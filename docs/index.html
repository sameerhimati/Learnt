FAQ

Is my data private?
Yes. All your learnings are stored locally on your device. We don't collect any data.

Does it work offline?
Yes. The app works entirely offline since everything is stored on your device.

What are the review intervals?
Based on neuroscience research: 1, 7, 16, and 35 days. Optimized for long-term retention.
