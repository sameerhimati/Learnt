Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All your learnings are stored locally on your device. We don't collect any data — no analytics, no tracking, no cloud.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The app works entirely offline since everything is stored on your device. Voice transcription uses Apple's on-device speech recognition.

What are the review intervals?

Based on neuroscience research: 1, 7, 16, and 35 days after you first capture a learning. Optimized for long-term retention.

Is it free?

Learnt is free during the beta period via TestFlight. Requires iOS 17 or later.

How do I share what I've learned?

Tap any learning to expand it, then use the share button to create a beautiful visual card you can send anywhere.
