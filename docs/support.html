Common Questions

How do I delete a learning?
Swipe left on the learning card, or tap to expand and use the delete option.

How do I edit a learning?
Tap the learning card to expand it, then tap the edit button.

Can I recover deleted learnings?
No. Deleted learnings are permanently removed since all data is stored locally.

How do I change the reminder time?
Go to You tab → Settings → Reminders to adjust capture and review times.

Why isn't voice transcription working?
Make sure you've granted microphone and speech recognition permissions in Settings → Learnt.

How do I share a learning?
Tap to expand any learning, then tap the share button to create a visual card.
