Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

Learnt is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe your personal learnings should remain personal.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers, databases, or any infrastructure that receives your data. Your learnings never leave your device.

What We Collect

Nothing. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No usage data collection
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Microphone Access

Learnt requests microphone access to enable voice entry. Audio is processed on-device using Apple's Speech framework for transcription. Audio recordings are not stored or transmitted anywhere.

Speech Recognition

Voice transcription uses Apple's on-device speech recognition. Your voice data is processed locally and is not sent to any servers.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, notification scheduling is handled entirely on your device. We do not use push notification services that would require sending data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Learnt does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. There are no analytics platforms, crash reporters, or advertising networks in the app.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, deleting the app removes all your data permanently. There is nothing stored elsewhere to delete.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at sam@itamih.com.
